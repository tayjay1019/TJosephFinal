Bulls and Cows

How to play:

In this game, the player will try to guess a four digit number with digits ranging form 1 to 9, no 0's.

  • There are NO repeat numbers
  • Each Cow means one of your digits is correct but in the wrong spot
  • Each Bull means one of your digits is both correct AND in the proper place
  • You get 4 Bulls and YOU WIN!
