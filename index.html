About Me

Hello! My name is Taylor Joseph.
I am currently a first semester student at Waukesha County Technical College. While I may not have the technical expertise, I greatly enjoy the challenge of game development, and even helped make a board game that was successfully published.

I was born in San Diego CA, and over the past 30 years I have been acquiring a variety of skills from various forms of employment, from construction, to janitorial, to working a front desk. Everywhere I have worked, I have gained valuable life experience.
